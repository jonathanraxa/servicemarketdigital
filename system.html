Marketing S.M.A.R.T. for you.

Let's figure out our goals together.

S - Specific

Being specific, what exactly do we want more of?

By February 1st, I want to have attracted 100 likes to my Facebook page by posting 2-3 times a day and responding to customer queries within 24 hours.

M - Measureable

Let's face it, we want tangibleresults. Let us outline measureable, and incremental objectives that you can track and focus on.

By April 1st, I will have increased my content sharing, clicking and commenting by 10% to help us reach a larger audience.

A - Attainable

We try to be realistic as possible. Marketing is a marathon, not a sprint, and we can achieve attainable goals, together.

Each week I will post 5 to 10 posts on Twitter and have a new Vimeo video each month.

R - Relevant/Result Focused

Focus on what your actions are meant to achieve; this could be increasing awareness of your brand and many other subtle goals.

By May 1st, I will develop a system that directly ties all social media activity to lead collection through giving a sign up reason for an exclusive newsletter where I will be delivering early-bird deals to customers.

T - Time Bound

Your social media marketing goals should be properly timed to create a sense of urgency and tension that pushes you to achieve your goals.
