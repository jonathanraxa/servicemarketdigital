Why Facebook?

Facebook is perhaps the number one arena for digital marketing. 1 in 5 page views come from Facebook each day alone! Just from that standpoint, think of the number of people you can reach and grow your market for your small business!

Why Instagram?

With Instagram, we can focus on using photos as stories. Does your image have something that it communicates about your brand? Instagram has a wide range of users and enables businesses to showcase a viewpoint based on the service.

Why YouTube?

Think of YouTube as a long form marketing media outlet where your message is spanned out from a few minutes to as long as well over a few hundred minutes. In our day in age, the popularity of visual processing is a very important element recently brought into our society.

Why Twitter?

Twitter is the testing grounds of your services, allowing us to communicate more extensively to the public. What does not work here may most likley not work on other platforms.

Why Vimeo?

Just like YouTube, Vimeo is a long message platform allowing us to use visuals as a means of communication. Though not as popular as YouTube, Vimeo gives us a nice, clean interface to promote your services which is ideal for showing videos on your website.

Why Snapchat?

Snapchat has taken social media by storm and mainly used by a younger audience. It allows us to tell a brand story and bring promotion if done properly.
